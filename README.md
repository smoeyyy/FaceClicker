# FaceClicker

In FaceClicker, click faces as they move across the screen to gain points. Clicking on a Smiley Face gains one point. Clicking on a Frowny Face loses one point. Pass 5 rounds of increasing difficulty to get a max score of 50.

Uses Phaser 3.80

Art assets from the [Shape Characters](https://kenney.nl/assets/shape-characters) asset pack by [Kenney Assets](https://kenney.nl/), with gratitude.
